Colette O'Beirne

Sculptor & Musician

Colette O'Beirne is an Irish sculptress, working in clay, ceramic and bronze. Colette takes the natural world and animal forms as the subject of many of her works. Through a delicate interplay of gesture and poise, she elicits the vital spirit latent in nature's in these biological forms. Originally from the East Wall, Dublin, Colette O'Beirne studied sculpture at the Academy of Art San Francisco, California, before founding her Dublin-based studio in 2017. She has had her work exhibited in the Spring Exhibition of the Palace of Fine Arts in San Francisco California, and in the Los Gatos Gallery in conjunction with the Los Gatos Irish Arts Festival.
